What is P-Score‚Ñ¢?

The P-Score‚Ñ¢ is a unified 0-100 score aggregating 10 equally weighted components for holistic website performance analysis.

  • Karpov (Speed & Performance): Load time, resources, optimizations.
  • Vortex (Accessibility): Alt tags, ARIA, headings (WCAG 2.1 AA).
  • Pulse (SEO & Social): Meta tags, OG, Twitter cards.
  • Helix (Privacy & Security): Trackers, headers, HTTPS.
  • Nexus (Mobile Responsiveness): Viewport, media queries, PWA.
  • Echo (Sustainability): Page weight, efficient formats, green hosting.
  • Nova (Infrastructure & Scalability): CDN, caching, compression.
  • Quantum (Best Practices & Code Quality): No deprecated, SRI, etc.
  • Aether (Modern Tech & Future-Readiness): WASM, modules, modern frameworks.
  • Eden (Efficiency & Page Weight): Raw size tiers.

P-Score = Average of all. 80+ Excellent; 60-79 Good; <60 Needs Work.

Compared to Google PageSpeed: Broader (adds privacy/sustainability); server-side only; cached; unified score.
